Nelson Mandela

About
  • Full Name: Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela
  • Born: July 18, 1918
  • Nationality: South African
  • Profession: Anti-apartheid revolutionary, President

Major Achievements

  1. Fought against apartheid in South Africa
  2. Spent 27 years in prison for his activism
  3. Became the first Black President of South Africa (1994)
  4. Won the Nobel Peace Prize (1993)

Legacy

  • Symbol of peace and reconciliation
  • Global icon for human rights
  • Inspired movements for equality worldwide
